Tropical Oak

Tropical Oak trees thrive in tropical and subtropical regions, playing a crucial role in forest ecosystems. Known for their dense foliage, they provide vital oxygen, support biodiversity, and help reduce deforestation.

Key Benefits:

  • Helps in carbon sequestration
  • Restores biodiversity and wildlife habitats
  • Provides shade and reduces soil erosion
  • Boosts air quality by producing oxygen

Tree Statistics

Age: 5 years

Height: 12 feet

Species: Quercus Tropicalis

Location: Southeast Asia
